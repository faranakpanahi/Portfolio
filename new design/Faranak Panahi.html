This is me — Faranak

A creative graphic and front-end designer based in Calgary, Canada. I have a strong passion for visual storytelling and bringing ideas to life through clean, modern design. With over 15 years of experience, I specialize in branding, UI/UX design, typography, and book cover design. I work with tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, CorelDraw, and After Effects to create visuals that are both beautiful and functional.
On the front-end side, I build responsive websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Figma, with some experience in Python as well. Design for me is not just about how things look, it’s about how they feel and work. I believe that simplicity in design leads to clarity, impact, and real connection. I enjoy collaborating with others, solving creative challenges, and turning complex ideas into powerful visual experiences.
